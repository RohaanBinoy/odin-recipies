Biriyani

Biriyani is one of the best dish available in Kerala. Everyone loves Biriyani. It is my personal favourite. If you Don't like Biriyani please DELETE yourself.

If you do love Biriyani, here is the recipie for it. Enjoy...

Ingedients Required:

  • Biriyani Rice
  • Chicken
  • Masala

Steps to make Biriyani:

  1. Take Chicken.
  2. Take Rice.
  3. Mix with masala
  4. Cook it for 30 mins.
  5. Congrats Your Biriyani is ready.
