BURGER

Burger is one of the best dish available in the world. Everyone loves Burger. It is my personal favourite. If you Don't like Burger please DELETE yourself.

If you do love Burger, here is the recipie for it. Enjoy...

Ingedients Required:

  • Bun
  • Patty
  • Sauce(any sauce will do)

Steps to make burger:

  1. Take patty.
  2. Put it inside the bun.
  3. Cook it for 20 mins.
  4. Put sauce.
  5. Congrats Your Burger is ready.
