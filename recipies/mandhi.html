MANDHI

Kuzhimandhi is the best dish available in Kerala. Everyone loves Kuzhimandhi. It is my personal favourite. If you Don't like Kuzhimandhi please DELETE yourself.

If you do love kuzhimandhi, here is the recipie for it. Enjoy...

Ingedients Required:

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Mayonaise

Steps to make mandhi:

  1. Take Chicken.
  2. Take Rice.
  3. Cook it for 20 mins.
  4. Put mayonaise.
  5. Congrats Your Mandhi is ready.
